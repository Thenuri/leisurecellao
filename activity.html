Hot Air Balloning


Hot air balloon is lighter-than-air aircraft that consists of an envelope carrying heated air. A wicker basket or gondola is suspended underneath the floor, transporting people and a source of heat, which is commonly an open flame made by burning liquid propane.

Take in the views and sounds of Sri Lanka from above as you fly over the air. At an average height of 500 to 2000 feet, float effortlessly above treetops, wildlife, lakes and rivers, cultural sites, local urban homesteads, and landmarks like as Sigiriya Rock. At daybreak, sunrise rides are offered to enhance the experience by mixing the sights and sounds of the morning with an unforgettable adventure. The sport of hot air ballooning is relatively new. Imagine effortlessly flying around Sri Lanka's stunning scenery.

Kitesurfing


 This is a wind-powered extreme sport in which the kiter drags a large power kite across a body of water, land, or snow. The activities include paragliding, surfing, windsurfing, skating, snowboarding, and wakeboarding. Kiteboarding is one of the most cost-effective and practical sailing hobbies available.


Why Choose Sri Lanka For Kitesurfing


Sri Lanka provides great kitesurfing conditions, with flat water, constant wind, and favorable wind for nine months of the year.


When Is The Best Time To Kitesurf In Sri Lanka?


The major kitesurfing season in Sri Lanka is from May to October. We look forward to days during the main season when the wind is constant and dependable. The wind climbs between 18 and 30 knots as a result of your participatory play with the wind, turning each day into an amazing kitesurfing adventure in its own right. In Sri Lanka, the months of December to March are also good for kitesurfing. The wind doesn't let us down in Kalpitiya throughout the winter, therefore we kiters from head to toe love these days on the lake. Even if the wind speed drops a little, you'll have steady days of wind between 14 to 18 mph.

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is a sport which is done underwater using breathing equipment. And to swim underwater they use fins so they can move easily. When you dive you can see beautiful corals and various types of ocean wonders.
Scuba diving in Sri Lanka is an incredible thing that you should go on because this island is blessed with many ocean creatures and it will be a memorable experience


When To Dive InSri Lanka


The ideal time to dive the west and south-west coasts is from October to May, and the best time to dive the north-east coast is from May to October. Sri Lanka has it all: fantastic diving, nice folks, delicious food, and top-side treasures.
